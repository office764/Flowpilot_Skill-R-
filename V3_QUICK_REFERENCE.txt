  5. QUALITY INSPECTOR - Consistency & realism

SEGMENT TYPES:
  TALKING - Avatar to camera (max 5 sec each)
  BROLL_PRODUCT - Product close-ups, no people (3-5 sec)
  BROLL_INTERACTION - Person using product (3-5 sec)
  BROLL_LIFESTYLE - Mood/lifestyle shots (3-5 sec)

TIME ALLOCATION:
  Total: 28-32 seconds across 6-8 segments
  TALKING: 3 segments (~15 sec) - Hook, Benefit, CTA
  BROLL_PRODUCT: 1-2 segments (~5 sec)
  BROLL_INTERACTION: 1-2 segments (~5 sec)
  BROLL_LIFESTYLE: 0-1 segment (~3 sec)

VIDEO STRUCTURE (7 STAGES):
  1. HOOK (0:00-0:04) - TALKING - Attention grab
  2. PRODUCT REVEAL (0:04-0:08) - BROLL_PRODUCT - Close-up
  3. USAGE DEMO (0:08-0:13) - BROLL_INTERACTION - Application
  4. BENEFIT EXPLANATION (0:13-0:18) - TALKING - Benefits
  5. TEXTURE/DETAIL (0:18-0:22) - BROLL - Sensory details
  6. RESULT/FEELING (0:22-0:26) - TALKING/BROLL - Satisfaction
  7. CTA (0:26-0:30) - TALKING - Call-to-action + price

HYPER-REALISM RULES (3 mandatory ending blocks):
  RULE 1: Avatar prompts (TALKING) - 400+ char ending
          ➜ "hyper-realistic footage indistinguishable from real camera..."
  RULE 2: BRoll prompts (PRODUCT/LIFESTYLE) - 350+ char ending
          ➜ "shot on Sony A7R V with Sony FE 85mm f/1.4 GM..."
  RULE 3: Interaction prompts (BROLL_INTERACTION) - 400+ char ending
          ➜ "hyper-realistic 4K footage of real person using product..."

FORBIDDEN WORDS AUTO-REPLACE:
  "smooth skin" → "textured skin with visible pores"
  "perfect face" → "naturally asymmetric human face"
  "beautiful lighting" → actual camera+lens specification
  "high quality" → "photorealistic DSLR photograph"
  "4K" alone → must include camera model + lens + ISO + color temp

PRODUCT INTERACTION GUIDE:
  토너/에센스/앰플 → gently patting onto cheek
  크림/로션 → scooping & spreading in circular motion
  미스트/분사형 → spraying with visible mist particles
  향수 → spraying on wrist pulse point
  디바이스 → pressing & circular motion on skin
  스틱형 → gliding along jawline
  펌프형 → pressing pump, product pooling

TTS SCRIPT RULES:
  Language: Korean Gen-Z female tone (20s)
  Style: Natural, conversational (NOT ad copy)
  Expressions: 진짜, 대박, 이거 찐이야, ~거든요, ~잖아요
  Length: 15-25 Korean characters per segment
  Emotions: excited, calm, surprised, satisfied, curious

OUTPUT SCHEMA FIELDS (all required):
  timeline_id (number: 1, 2, 3...)
  section (hook/product_reveal/usage_demo/benefit/texture/result/cta)
